Biography

Received a Ph.D. degree in computer science from Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Science (CAS), Beijing, in Jun. 2020. Now he is a assistant professor in School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, Nanjing, China. His research interests cover computer vision, pattern recognition and machine learning, especially focusing on face and facial expression anslysis.
